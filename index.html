The question

Participants

Order is the relay order. The URL powers the one-click launch each turn. Each slot starts with its recommended role preselected in the dropdown; pick a different suggestion, choose (no role), or type your own, and it gets woven into that model's prompt. Your roster is remembered between sessions.

How they talk

One round = everyone speaks once. More rounds means they respond to each other, but every turn re-reads the transcript, so watch the token counter.

Presets

A preset stores your roster, mode, rounds, and format under a name. It never stores your question or any answers. Saved locally in this browser only.

Transcript

The text here is the answer you captured. Trimming what's sent forward never alters this copy or the exports; it only changes what later models receive. Re-pasting a turn overwrites its captured text.

Relay Console v runs in your browser and never uploads your questions, answers, or session data. It opens a provider website only when you explicitly choose Copy & open. Anything you paste into that provider is handled under its own terms.
Autosave keeps your session when you run this as a saved .html file. Inside a preview it may not persist, so use Save file for a hard backup.
